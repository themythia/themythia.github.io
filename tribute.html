ELON MUSK

Elon Musk, the man behind SpaceX, Tesla, OpenAI and many more.


Here's a short timeline of Elon Musk's life:

  • 1971 - Born to engineer father and model mother
  • 1983 - Writes video game Blastar, sells it for $500
  • 1989 - Starts college in Ontario
  • 1994 - Gets Wharton economics degree
  • 1995 - Starts Zip2 with brother, coding it himself
  • 1996 - Raises first investment money ($3m)
  • 1999 - Gets $22m from sale of Zip2
  • 1999 - Starts X.com - an online bank with his own money
  • 2000 - Merges with Confinity, forming Paypal
  • 2000 - Gets ousted as CEO from Paypal
  • 2002 - Starts SpaceX to build his own rockets
  • 2004 - Invests in Tesla
  • 2006 - His first ever rocket launches and explodes
  • 2006 - Invests in SolarCity
  • 2008 - First Tesla customers get their Roadsters
  • 2008 - Succeeds to launch his 4th rocket
  • 2011 - Promises to send humans to Mars by 2012 - 2031
  • 2011 - Announces reusable rockets
  • 2012 - Dragon docks with the ISS
  • 2015 - Rocket landing succeeds
  • 2017 - Succeds to launch Falcon Heavy and sends his Roadster to space

Elon Musk
